READ CAREFULLY to understand CSS positioning!!

Recall that elements with position: absolute can be positioned with the top, left, bottom, and right properties, like a coordinate system where (0, 0) is at the top left corner. However, (0, 0) is not always the top left corner of the whole screen. It is actually the parent element that has position: relative.

In our code, .image-card is the parent of .image-card-color-overlay, and we want to position the color overlay inside the overall card, with the top left corner of the card as the (0, 0) coordinate.
